The Empathy Project

building empathy, and connecting you to stories from the heartland


The Empathy Project is an initiative by Veer Sagar to profile incredibly inspiring stories of hope, progress and human achievement. We strongly believe that building empathy is the first step to truly connecting with someone. As people of privilege, it's our responsibility and solemn duty to empathize with vulnerable communities, support them and amplify their stories . The Empathy Project will profile several stories. To begin with, we will take you on a journey of an athlete in India. We need to see and understand what an Indian sportsperson goes through. The struggles, the trials and the tribulations. And we need to channel that empathy into action and support them in every way possible .
